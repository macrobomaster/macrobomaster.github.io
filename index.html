BUILD THE FUTURE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND ROBOTICS






RoboMaster Robotics Competition focuses on the comprehensive application and engineering practice ability of the participating members in science and engineering disciplines, fully integrating many robotics-related technical disciplines such as "machine vision," "embedded systems design," "mechanical and electrical assembly," "inertial navigation," "human-computer interaction," and so on. The innovative combination of E-sports presentation with live competition makes the stakes both fun and intense, drawing in technology enthusiasts and the general public alike. The competition is for university students and runs annually from the start of the school year to the end of summer.

About Us

MAC RoboMaster is a team of highly motivated and passionate students from McMaster University located in Ontario, Canada. Our team name is the Mac Falcons. We participate in the yearly RoboMaster Robotics Competition where we compete against universities all over the world. Each season, the team is tasked with developing a group of remote-control and autonomous battle robots from the ground up. This multidisciplinary project is no small task, but we love what we do and are committed to learning and developing as future professionals. We come from many faculties within engineering as well as business, and we strive for excellence every day.



Computer Vision

The "eyes and brain" of our robots, utilizing object detection and decision-making powered by AI for better robot behaviour.

Mechanical Design

The "skeleton" of our robots, implementing frame design to improve their strength and stability.

Embedded Systems

The "nervous system" of our robots, optimizing the control logic and communication between components for better robot performance.

Electrical Design

The "heart and blood vessels" of our robots, ensuring reliable power distribution and efficient energy management.
