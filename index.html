ABOUT

MAC RoboMaster is a student-run team at McMaster designing and manufacturing RC and fully-autonomous robots to compete in the yearly RoboMaster Robotics Competition.

 

Sponsored and supported by DJI, the worlds leader in civilian drones and aerial imaging technology, RoboMaster is an annual robotics competition for teams of aspiring engineers to design and build next-generation robots to compete for supremacy on the RoboMaster battlefield. RoboMaster has gained popularity among the engineering community and has provided a world stage for University students to pursue their passion in science and technology, and to showcase their talent.
